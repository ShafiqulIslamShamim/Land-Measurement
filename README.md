Simplify your land calculations with the ultimate Land Measurement app! Whether you are a landowner, a real estate agent, or a surveyor, this app provides accurate area calculations and unit conversions instantly.

Designed with a modern, user-friendly interface, our app helps you calculate land area based on shape and convert between local and international units seamlessly.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📐 Shape-Based Calculation
No need to stress over complex geometry. Just select the shape of your land and get accurate area measurements instantly:

- **Quadrilateral**  
  Calculate rectangular, square, and scalene (irregular) plots with ease.

- **Circular**  
  Ideal for round plots, ponds, or tanks.

- **Triangular**  
  Quickly measure triangular sections or corner plots.

---

### 📏 Comprehensive Unit Support
Input measurements in the units you’re comfortable with:

**Supported Input Units**
- Feet  
- Inch  
- Meter  
- Cubit  
- Yard  
- Centimeter  
- Millimeter  

**Instant Results In**

- **International Units**
  - Square Feet  
  - Square Meter  
  - Acre  
  - Hectare  

- **Regional (Standard) Units**
  - Shotok (Decimal)  
  - Katha  
  - Bigha  

- **Regional (Localized) Units**
  - Kora  
  - Joistho  
  - Kani  

---

### 🔄 Advanced Unit Converter
Need fast and accurate unit conversions? The built-in converter makes it simple and reliable.

- Instantly convert between **Shotok, Katha, Bigha, Acre, Hectare**, and other supported units  
- Enter a value in any unit and automatically see the equivalent values in all available units (**Feet, Inch, Meter, Cubit, Yard, Centimeter, Millimeter**)  
- Supports both **land area** and **linear measurements** for complete flexibility  
- Designed for quick use with clear and easy-to-read results  

---

### 📏 Side-Based Calculation
Need to know the measurements of all sides quickly? Just enter the length of one side and get instant conversions.

- Available for **Quadrilateral, Circular, and Triangular** shapes  
- Enter one side length and automatically get results for other sides  
- Shows converted values in **Feet, Inch, Meter, Cubit, Yard, Centimeter, Millimeter**  
- Fast, simple, and perfect for practical land measurements  

---

### 💾 Calculation History Log
Never lose track of your measurements. The built-in local persistence engine keeps all your activities stored safely:

- **Saved Calculation History**  
  Every shape calculation or unit conversion is automatically logged with date and time tags.
- **Easy Re-Entry & Re-Calculation**  
  Tap on any history entry to instantly restore and reload the input parameters back into the active calculation screen.
- **Swipe-to-Delete Management**  
  Easily clean up or reorganize your list of saved measurements.

Perfect for land measurement, planning, and everyday conversion needs.

2. Automatically Collected Information (Firebase Services)

The app uses Google Firebase, a service provided by Google LLC.

A. Firebase Analytics

Purpose:
- To understand general app usage patterns
- To improve app features and user experience

Data Collected (Non-Personal):
- App interaction events (screen views, feature usage)
- Device information (model, OS version)
- App version
- Approximate location (country-level only)

Important Notes:
- No personal or academic data is collected
- Data is aggregated and anonymized
- Google processes this data according to its privacy policy

------------------------------------------------------------

B. Firebase Crashlytics

Purpose:
- To detect, monitor, and fix app crashes and errors

Data Collected (Non-Personal):
- Crash logs and stack traces
- Device type and operating system
- App state during a crash

Important Notes:
- Crash reports do not intentionally contain personal data
- Data is used only to improve app stability

------------------------------------------------------------

The app processes information only when the user actively provides it.

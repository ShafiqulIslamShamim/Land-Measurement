Simplify your land calculations with the ultimate Land Measurement app! Whether you are a landowner, a real estate agent, or a surveyor, this app provides accurate area calculations and unit conversions instantly.

Designed with a modern, user-friendly interface, our app helps you calculate land area based on shape and convert between local and international units seamlessly.

### üìê Shape-Based Calculation
No need to stress over complex geometry. Just select the shape of your land and get accurate area measurements instantly:

- **Quadrilateral**  
  Calculate rectangular, square, and scalene (irregular) plots with ease.
- **Circular**  
  Ideal for round plots, ponds, or tanks.
- **Triangular**  
  Quickly measure triangular sections or corner plots.
